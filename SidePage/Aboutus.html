About Pleasant Lake Medical Center

Welcome, Pleasant Lake Medical, where our Board Certified Family Medicine Practitioners can provide care for your family.

Our family medicine team, comprised of physicians, nurse practitioners, and licensed practical nurses, is focused on you and your family's total health. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive medical care, as well as prevention and wellness services for every member of your family.

Pleasant Lake Family Medical providers are committed to developing long-term relationships with their patients and see and treat the whole person and helping patients live longer healthier lives. Through this partnership, our physicians can help you prevent, understand, and manage illness, navigate the health system, and set health goals.

We value communication and patient collaboration; we are here to listen to your concerns, address your questions, and provide education on your health. Our commitment and medical expertise assure you receive the highest level of quality care in an environment that is family-friendly, with respect, compassion, and empathy for each individual patient. We would be delighted to have you and your family join our practice.

We offer many sick visits throughout the day for non-life-threatening issues. We always have a provider on-call after hours to help with the sudden illness or concern that you may have. When you leave our office, we would like you to have a well-informed and positive experience. We are always here for the needs of your family.
